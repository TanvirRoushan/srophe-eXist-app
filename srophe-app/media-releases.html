Media Contacts

We welcome inquiries from the media and are happy to arrange contact or interviews with the editors of Syriaca.org.

Journalists/members of the media should contact the general editor and project director, Dr. David Michelson, at info@syriaca.org.

Follow Syriaca.org on Social Media

Those interested in receiving regular updates about Syriaca.org are encouraged to follow the project on Twitter @srophe or to subscribe to Syriaca.org blog: http://syriaca.org/blog/. An RSS feed is also available: http://syriaca.org/blog/feed/.

Press Releases and Reports

Syriaca.org is preparing several online reference works. Media releases related to each publication will be posted here.

    Report on the Destruction of Syriac Cultural Heritage in the Contemporary Middle East
    As a result of ongoing civil war and political chaos, the Syriac and Aramaic-heritage communities of the Middle East are under unprecedented pressure. This report provides an introduction to both terrible human cost of the current conflicts and to the ways in which Syriac and Aramaic physical and cultural heritage are gravely threatened. Syriaca.org exists to address this challenge by preserving and recording the collective cultural heritage of Syriac heritage communities around the globe. This report can be downloaded here.
    Press Release Announcing The Syriac Gazetteer to the Scholarly Community
    Syriaca.org is pleased to announce publication of The Syriac Gazetteer, a geographical dictionary for Syriac Studies. A press release with information for scholarly audiences and contributors can be downloaded here.
